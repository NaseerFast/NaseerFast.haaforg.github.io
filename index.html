Who We Are

At HAAF, our mission is to empower communities through holistic education and humanitarian interventions, fostering sustainable development and a future filled with hope and awareness.

What We Do

HAAF delivers various services to achieve its mission and goal, including

  • Strategic Goal 1

    Education Excellence

  • Strategic Goal 2

    Integrated Humanitarian Intervention

  • Strategic Goal 3

    Geographical Expansion

  • Strategic Goal 4

    Capacity Building

  • Strategic Goal 5

    Advocacy and Awareness

  • IDP/Refugee/Returnees Support Program

    Integrated Humanitarian Program is one of our strategic goal to ensure people and communities affected by natural disaster and man-made conflicts have receive a Holistic Programs Addressing Community Needs and Strengthen Emergency Response.

  • School Enrollment Program

    Education Excellence aims to improve teaching methods and curriculum development to increase access to education and school enrollment.

  • Community Empowerment Program

    Empowering Community Members through Skill-Building Programs to Promote Self-Reliance, Including Educational Skills.
